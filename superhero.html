My Favorite Superhero: The Doctor

The Doctor doesn't exactly have an undercover name, but 'The Doctor' isn't his real name. However, no one (except River Song and the Doctor himself) knows what it is. Some people say that it is a math equation, and The Doctor has joked about 'Basil' before. But these are just two of the many names mentioned on the show. No matter what it is, it's in Gallifreyan, so we wouldn't be able to read or pronounce it anyways.
